Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the ECS Site Manager Card, you should already be working in a senior role on UK construction sites and meet the ECS management card criteria below.

  • Hold a relevant ECS Gold Card or equivalent Level 3+ electrical qualification in your discipline.
  • Completed a UK-recognised management or health & safety qualification such as:
    • CCNSG / CITB Site Management Safety Training Scheme (SMSTS)
    • IOSH Managing Safely in Construction
    • NEBOSH National or Construction Diploma
    • Other equivalent UK construction management qualification
  • Passed the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment or hold an accepted exemption.
  • Valid proof of identity (UK or international Passport, photocard Driving Licence or government-issued ID).
  • Demonstrable recent experience in electrical site management on UK construction projects.

Required Documentation

When you apply online for your ECS Site Manager Card, you will normally be asked to upload clear copies of the following documents:

  • Completed ECS Site Manager Card application form.
  • Proof of ECS Gold Card or equivalent technical qualification.
  • Evidence of management or health & safety training (SMSTS, IOSH, NEBOSH etc.).
  • Proof of ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment pass (or exemption).
  • One recent passport-style photograph.
  • Proof of ID – passport, photocard driving licence or government-issued ID card.
  • Updated CV or short portfolio outlining your electrical site management experience.

Management Focus

The ECS Site Manager Card is built around day-to-day life on a construction site. It is meant for electrical site managers who control permits to work, oversee subcontractors, organise toolbox talks and ensure that every task is completed safely and in line with UK regulations.

By holding this electrical site manager card, you show main contractors and clients that you can balance programme, quality and safety while leading an on-site team on complex electrical projects.

ECS Site Manager Card – Application Process

  1. Gather proof of your ECS Gold Card or equivalent electrical qualification.
  2. Make sure your management or health & safety qualification evidence is up to date.
  3. Pass the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment if you have not already done so.
  4. Complete the ECS Site Manager Card application form and upload your documents online.
  5. Pay the £79 + VAT service fee securely through our UK-based payment system.
  6. Once approved, your ECS Site Manager Card is normally issued within 15–20 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions – ECS Site Manager Card

What qualifications are accepted for the Site Manager Card?

Accepted qualifications for the ECS Site Manager Card include CCNSG / CITB Site Management Safety Training Scheme (SMSTS), IOSH Managing Safely in Construction, NEBOSH National or Construction Diploma or another equivalent UK-recognised management or health & safety course. You must also hold a suitable ECS Gold Card or equivalent electrical qualification in your trade.

How does the Site Manager Card differ from other ECS management cards?

The ECS Site Manager Card is focused on the work of an electrical site manager based on construction sites, rather than office-only project roles. It highlights your ability to supervise teams, implement method statements, manage risk assessments and keep day-to-day site operations safe and compliant with UK regulations.

Is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment required for this card?

Yes. In most cases you will need a valid ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment pass to obtain the ECS Site Manager Card, unless you hold an accepted exemption. This ensures every card holder has up-to-date health, safety and environmental knowledge relevant to construction sites in the UK.

Are there discounts available for the application fee?

Yes, discounts on the ECS Site Manager Card fee may be available for JIB and union members, depending on current agreements. The standard online service fee for this electrical site manager card is £79 + VAT, but you should confirm the latest price and any discounts with your employer, trade body or the ECS guidance at the time you apply.
