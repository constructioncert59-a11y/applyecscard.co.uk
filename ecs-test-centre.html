About the ECS Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Test

The ECS Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) Assessment is the standard test used in the electrotechnical industry to show that you understand key health, safety and environmental requirements before working on site with an ECS card.

How to Use This ECS Test Centre Finder

Start typing the town, city, centre name or postcode in the search box above. The list will filter in real time, so you can quickly see which ECS test centres are closest to where you live or work.
