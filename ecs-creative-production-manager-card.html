Eligibility Requirements

  • Experience managing creative production or technical teams on site.
  • Comprehensive understanding of production safety principles and safe systems of work.
  • Currently working in, or seeking, creative production management or supervisory roles.
  • Successful completion of the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment or accepted exemption.
  • Ability to lead, brief and supervise creative and technical teams during builds, rehearsals and live operations.

Required Documentation

  • Proof of identity (valid passport or photocard driving licence).
  • Proof of current address dated within the last three months.
  • Evidence of creative production management or on-site supervisory experience.
  • One recent passport-style photograph.
  • ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment certificate (where applicable).

Application Process

  1. Provide your personal details and employment/role information in creative production.
  2. Pass the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment at an approved test centre, if not already held.
  3. Submit evidence of creative production management or supervisory experience.
  4. Upload your supporting documents and complete the online application form.
  5. Your application and documents are checked and verified by the relevant ECS body.
  6. Once approved, your ECS Creative Production Manager Card is produced and dispatched within 15–20 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

Once we receive your complete application with all required documents, it typically takes around 15–20 working days to process and dispatch your ECS Creative Production Manager Card. Incomplete or incorrect applications may experience delays.

What is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment?

This mandatory assessment checks your understanding of essential health, safety and environmental responsibilities on UK sites. It must normally be passed before a new or renewed ECS Creative Production Manager Card can be issued.

What types of creative production does this card cover?

The card can be relevant for events, exhibitions, film and TV production, live performances, creative installations and other projects where production management, coordination and on-site safety responsibilities are required.

How do I renew my card before expiry?

You can usually apply to renew your card up to six months before its expiry date. Renewal generally requires a valid ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment and continued evidence of your creative production management experience.
