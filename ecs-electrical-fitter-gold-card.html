Eligibility Requirements

  • Relevant electrical fitting qualification (e.g. NVQ/SVQ Level 3 or equivalent)
  • Proven practical experience in electrical fitting and system maintenance
  • Passed the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment
  • Typically 3+ years experience in electrical fitting roles
  • Valid proof of identity (Passport, Driving Licence, or Government-issued ID)

Required Documentation

  • Completed ECS application form
  • Proof of qualifications (NVQ/SVQ Level 3 Electrical Fitting or equivalent)
  • One recent passport-size photograph
  • Proof of ID (Passport or Driving Licence)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement or similar, dated within last 3 months)

Training & Assessment

Approved UK training providers offer advanced electrical fitting courses and ECS Health, Safety & Environmental assessments. These can be completed through registered training centres or via approved test centres before applying for your ECS Electrical Fitter Gold Card.

Application Process

  1. Confirm that your qualifications and experience meet ECS/JIB requirements for Electrical Fitter (Gold card level).
  2. Pass the current ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment.
  3. Gather all required documents (ID, address, qualifications, photo).
  4. Submit your application online through ApplyECSCard or via your employer/training provider.
  5. We check your documents and submit your ECS application for processing.
  6. Receive your ECS Electrical Fitter Gold Card – usually within 10–15 working days once approved.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Electrical Fitter Gold Card application take?

Once we receive your complete application and all required documents, processing typically takes around 10–15 working days. Incomplete or incorrect applications may take longer to be approved.

Do I need to pass an ECS Health & Safety test?

Yes. A valid ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment pass is mandatory for all ECS gold card applications, including Electrical Fitter. The test confirms that you understand UK site safety rules and best practices.

How long is the ECS Electrical Fitter Gold Card valid?

The Electrical Fitter Gold Card is normally valid for 3 years. After expiry, you must renew the card by updating your documents and passing a new ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment.

What qualifications do I need for this ECS Gold Card?

You will generally need an NVQ/SVQ Level 3 in electrical fitting or an equivalent qualification that matches ECS/JIB requirements, plus suitable experience in electrical fitting roles.
