Automated Gate Safety Certification

The Gate Safe Installer Card is for professionals who have completed Gate Safe IOSH-approved training and passed the required ECS Health, Safety & Environment and ECS Electrical Safety Unit assessments. It confirms that the holder understands the hazards associated with automated gates and is competent to carry out risk assessments and safe installations.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Completion of Gate Safe training (IOSH-approved) within the last 2 years.
  • Pass the ECS Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) assessment.
  • Pass the ECS Electrical Safety Unit assessment for safe working near electrical systems.
  • Valid proof of identity – Passport, Driving Licence, or other accepted Government ID.

How to Apply

  1. Book and complete your Gate Safe training (IOSH-approved online course).
  2. Pass the Gate Safe training assessment and receive your IOSH certificate and Gate Safe membership ID.
  3. Complete the ECS Health, Safety & Environment and ECS Electrical Safety Unit assessments.
  4. Return to ECSApply and complete our online Gate Safe Installer Card application form.
  5. Upload your ID, proof of address, Gate Safe training evidence and ECS assessment passes.
  6. We submit and track your application – your card is usually issued within 10–15 working days after approval.

Card Validity & Renewal

  • The Gate Safe Installer Card is valid for 12 months from the date of training.
  • Refresher Gate Safe training is required every 2 years to maintain membership and eligibility.
  • You must keep your ECS HSE assessment up to date for renewals.
  • Pay the applicable card renewal fee through ECSApply and submit updated evidence when requested.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the Gate Safe Installer Card cost?

The Gate Safe training itself is typically £99 + VAT per delegate, paid directly to Gate Safe or the training provider. Our ECSApply service fee to help apply, renew or replace your card is £79 + VAT.

How long is the Gate Safe training and what does it cover?

The training usually lasts 4–4.5 hours including breaks. It covers automated gate safety requirements, applicable standards, risk assessment procedures, common hazard scenarios and best practice for installation, inspection and maintenance.

How long is the Gate Safe Installer Card valid for?

The Gate Safe Installer Card is valid for 12 months from the date of training. To keep your status current, you must complete Gate Safe refresher training every 2 years and keep your ECS HSE assessment valid.

What assessments do I need to pass?

You must complete the Gate Safe IOSH-approved training and pass two ECS assessments: the ECS Health, Safety & Environment assessment and the ECS Electrical Safety Unit assessment.

Can I take the Gate Safe training online?

Yes, Gate Safe training is delivered via an online interactive session, so you can attend from anywhere with a reliable internet connection and suitable device.
