Professional Questions & Answers

What identification is required?

+

You must bring original photo ID - either a valid UK driving licence or passport. Photocopies or digital copies are not accepted.

How soon can I book a test?

+

We typically have availability within 3-5 working days. Emergency same-day bookings may be available - please email us to check.

Is the centre wheelchair accessible?

+

Yes, our Leicester centre is fully accessible with dedicated parking, ramp access, and accessible testing facilities.

Can I reschedule my test?

+

Yes, you can reschedule free of charge with 48 hours notice. Contact us via email to rearrange your appointment.

What's included in the test fee?

+

The fee includes the computer-based test, use of facilities, immediate results, and your pass certificate for ECS card application.

How do I prepare for the test?

+

We provide access to official CITB revision materials. We recommend studying the health, safety and environmental topics covered in the CITB revision guide.
