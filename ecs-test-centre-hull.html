Frequently Asked Questions - ECS Test Hull

What is the ECS test in Hull? +

The ECS test is the Health, Safety and Environment assessment required for ECS card applications. It is taken at authorised centres in Hull and provides instant results.

How long does the ECS test take? +

The ECS Health & Safety test usually takes around 45 minutes. Results are available immediately after completion.

Can I book an ECS test online in Hull? +

Yes. You can book your ECS test online in Hull by selecting your preferred centre and time slot. Once confirmed, you will receive your booking details by email.

Do I need the ECS test for an ECS card? +

Yes, most ECS card applications require a valid Health, Safety and Environment test pass. Your certificate is used as part of the application process.

What ID do I need for the ECS test? +

You must bring a valid photo ID such as a passport or driving licence. Some centres may accept additional ID requirements.
