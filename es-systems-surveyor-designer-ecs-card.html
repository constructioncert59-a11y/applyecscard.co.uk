Eligibility Requirements

  • Completion of an approved qualification in Emergency & Security Systems design and/or surveying.
  • Comprehensive knowledge of E&S systems design principles and relevant UK standards.
  • Currently employed in, or seeking, E&S systems design or surveying roles within the UK.
  • Successful completion of the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment or an accepted exemption.
  • Relevant professional experience in emergency and security system design, surveying or project engineering.

Required Documentation

  • Proof of identity (valid passport or photocard driving licence).
  • Proof of address dated within the last three months.
  • Emergency & Security systems design/survey qualification certificates.
  • One recent passport-style photograph.
  • ECS HSE Assessment certificate or accepted exemption, where applicable.

Application Process

  1. Apply through this website and get a free mock test to help you prepare and pass your ECS assessment with confidence.
  2. Complete the relevant E&S systems design/survey qualifications within the FESS framework.
  3. Pass the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment (if required).
  4. Complete the E&S Systems Surveyor / Designer ECS Card application form online and upload your documents.
  5. Submit your application and pay the £79 + VAT service fee.
  6. Once approved, your card is normally produced and dispatched within 15–20 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions – E&S Systems Surveyor / Designer ECS Card

How long does the application process take?

Once we receive your complete application with all required documents, it typically takes around 15–20 working days to process and dispatch your E&S Systems Surveyor / Designer ECS Card. Incomplete applications may experience delays.

What is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment?

This mandatory assessment ensures you understand key health, safety and environmental requirements on UK sites. It must usually be passed before a new or renewed ECS card can be issued, unless you hold an accepted exemption.

What types of emergency and security systems does this card cover?

The E&S Systems Surveyor / Designer ECS Card can cover fire detection and alarm systems, emergency lighting, access control, intruder alarms, CCTV and other life safety and security systems designed and surveyed in line with UK standards and client specifications.

How do I renew my card before expiry?

You can typically renew your card up to six months before its expiry date. Renewal follows similar steps to the initial application and may require evidence of ongoing professional development and continued work in E&S systems design or surveying, along with a valid ECS HSE Assessment where needed.
