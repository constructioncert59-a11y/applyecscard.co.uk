Eligibility for the ECS Academically Qualified Person Card

To qualify for an ECS Academically Qualified Person Card you will usually need a combination of recognised academic achievement and a suitable understanding of health and safety. Criteria can vary depending on the exact route, but in general applicants should:

  • Hold a relevant academic qualification such as NVQ Level 3, HNC, HND, BSc, BEng or an equivalent recognised award
  • Have a good understanding of electrical safety principles and safe working on construction sites
  • Be currently working in, or seeking a role in, the UK construction or building services industry
  • Hold a valid pass in the ECS Health, Safety and Environmental Assessment where this is required for the AQP route

Documents You Will Usually Need

Having the right documents ready will help your ECS Academically Qualified Person Card application go through more smoothly. You will normally be asked to provide:

  • Proof of identity, such as a valid passport or photocard driving licence
  • Proof of your current UK address dated within the last 3 months (for example a utility bill or bank statement)
  • Certified copies or clear scans of your academic qualification certificates that support your AQP application
  • A recent, clear passport-style photograph that meets ECS identification guidelines
  • A valid ECS Health, Safety and Environmental Assessment certificate, if required for your ECS Academically Qualified Person Card route

How the AQP ECS Application Process Works

The application process for the ECS Academically Qualified Person Card is designed to confirm that your academic record is genuine and that you understand how to work safely on UK construction sites. A typical journey looks like this:

  1. Check that your degree, HNC, HND, NVQ Level 3 or equivalent academic qualification is suitable for the AQP ECS route.
  2. Book and pass the ECS Health, Safety and Environmental Assessment if you do not already hold a valid pass.
  3. Gather your identity documents, proof of UK address and certified academic qualification certificates.
  4. Complete the online application form for the ECS Academically Qualified Person Card, or ask your employer to submit it on your behalf.
  5. ECS reviews your academic qualifications, checks your documents and confirms that you meet the criteria for an AQP ECS card.
  6. Once payment has been received and approved, your ECS Academically Qualified Person Card is printed and posted to your UK address.

ECS Academically Qualified Person Card – Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the ECS Academically Qualified Person Card application process take?

Once a complete ECS Academically Qualified Person Card application is received with all of the required documents, processing generally takes around 15–20 working days. If anything is missing, unclear or needs further checks, this can add extra time before the AQP ECS card is printed and dispatched to you.

What is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment?

The ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment is a computer-based test that checks your knowledge of safe working practices, site rules and environmental responsibilities on UK construction sites. Most routes to the ECS Academically Qualified Person Card require a valid pass in this assessment before the card can be issued or renewed.

Can I use my AQP card as an electrical operative card?

The ECS Academically Qualified Person Card recognises your academic qualifications and understanding of safety, but it may not be sufficient for roles that require a specific skilled ECS card, such as an Installation or Gold Card. Always check the exact ECS card type required by your employer or the main contractor for the work you are doing.

How do I renew my ECS Academically Qualified Person Card before it expires?

You can normally start the renewal process for your ECS Academically Qualified Person Card several months before the expiry date shown on the card. You will be asked to confirm that your details are still correct, provide any updated information, and make sure that your ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment is valid where required. Renewing in good time helps you avoid any gaps in your ECS AQP status.
