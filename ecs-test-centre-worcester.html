ECS Health, Safety & Environment Test in Worcester

What is the ECS HSE Test?

The ECS Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) test is a computer-based assessment required for most ECS card applications. It checks your knowledge of UK construction site safety, electrical hazards, PPE, manual handling, working at height and site regulations.
