The ECS test is a Health, Safety and Environment assessment required for ECS card applications. It is completed at authorised test centres and checks safety knowledge relevant to electrotechnical work.

The ECS Health, Safety and Environment test usually takes around 45 minutes and results are available immediately after completion.
