How the AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator Process Works

Exact application routes may vary, but most AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator journeys follow a similar pattern:

  1. Review AVIXA guidance to confirm that the Commercial AV Integrator credential fits your role and experience.
  2. Collect details of relevant projects, AV training and commercial AV integrator responsibilities.
  3. Complete the online AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator application with accurate information.
  4. Upload supporting documents, such as training records and project references, where requested.
  5. Pay the application fee and wait for your Commercial AV Integrator application to be assessed.
  6. Once approved, you receive confirmation of your AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator status and documentation.

AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator – Frequently Asked Questions

What is an AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator?

An AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator is a professional who brings together displays, audio, control, networking and conferencing into a working AV system for commercial environments. The role covers installation, configuration, testing, commissioning and basic user support, typically on UK business, education, retail and hospitality projects.

Do I need AVIXA training before applying?

Formal AVIXA training is highly beneficial but not always mandatory. You should, however, have solid hands-on experience as a commercial AV integrator and be able to evidence the projects and responsibilities you have taken on. Relevant AVIXA courses can strengthen your application and support your long-term CPD.

Is an AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator credential enough for UK construction sites?

The AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator credential proves your AV skills and knowledge, but some UK construction sites will still require CSCS, ECS or other site access cards. Many AV engineers therefore hold both an AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator credential and a suitable site safety card for UK projects.

Can I upgrade from commercial AV integrator to a more advanced AV role?

Yes. Many professionals use the AVIXA Commercial AV Integrator credential as a foundation for further development into AV system design, project management, programming or consultancy. Adding experience, CPD and additional certifications can help you progress to senior AV roles over time.
