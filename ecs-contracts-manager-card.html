Eligibility Criteria

  • Holds a relevant ECS Gold Card or equivalent professional qualifications in a related discipline.
  • Completed recognised management, contract or health & safety training such as:
    • CCNSG Construction Management courses
    • NEBOSH National or Construction Diploma
    • IOSH Managing Safely or equivalent
  • Demonstrable experience in construction contract management roles on UK projects.
  • Comprehensive knowledge of construction regulations and contract law relevant to your role.

Required Documentation

  • Proof of identity (valid Passport or photocard Driving Licence).
  • Proof of address dated within the last 3 months (utility bill, bank statement etc.).
  • Evidence of relevant contract management qualifications and professional experience.
  • Recent passport-style photograph suitable for printing on your ECS card.
  • Certificates for management and Health & Safety training (e.g. NEBOSH, IOSH, CCNSG).

Application Process

  1. Gather proof of your ECS Gold Card (or equivalent), contract management qualifications and Health & Safety training.
  2. Take the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment if you do not hold an accepted exemption.
  3. Complete the ECS Contracts Manager Card application form with accurate contact and employment details.
  4. Upload or provide copies of all supporting documents for verification.
  5. Pay the standard ECS application fee (typically £79; some JIB/union members may receive discounts).
  6. Once approved, your ECS Contracts Manager Card is produced and dispatched, usually within 15–20 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions – ECS Contracts Manager Card

How long does the application process take?

Once we receive your complete application with all required documents, it typically takes around 15–20 working days to process and dispatch your ECS Contracts Manager Card. If information is missing or needs clarification, this can lead to delays.

What qualifications are required for the Contracts Manager Card?

You will normally need an ECS Gold Card or equivalent professional qualifications, together with recognised management, contract or health & safety training such as CCNSG Construction Management courses, NEBOSH Diplomas or IOSH Managing Safely, plus evidence of suitable contracts management experience.

Is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment mandatory?

The ECS HSE Assessment may be required if you do not hold an accepted equivalent Health & Safety qualification. It confirms that you understand key health, safety and environmental responsibilities when managing construction contracts on UK sites.

Are there discounts available for the application fee?

The standard application fee is usually around £79. However, discounts may apply for JIB or trade union members, depending on your membership and employer arrangements. You should check with your union, JIB or employer to confirm whether any reduced fees are available.
