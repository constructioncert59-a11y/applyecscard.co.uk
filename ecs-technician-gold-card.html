Eligibility Requirements

  • Holds an NVQ/SVQ Level 3 (or higher) in an electrotechnical discipline or an equivalent recognised qualification.
  • Current pass in the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment at the required level.
  • Typically a minimum of 5 years’ experience in electrotechnical roles, such as installation, maintenance, testing or design.
  • Demonstrated responsibility for fault-finding, decision-making or overseeing complex electrical work.
  • Valid proof of identity – Passport, UK Driving Licence, or Government-issued photo ID.

Required Documentation

  • Completed ECS application form (we help you complete this correctly via Apply ECS Card).
  • Copies of your NVQ/SVQ Level 3 or equivalent Technician-level qualifications.
  • Proof of a valid ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment pass.
  • One recent passport-style photograph that meets ECS requirements.
  • Proof of ID (Passport or Driving Licence), and proof of address if requested by ECS.

Who Is the Technician Gold Card For?

This card is ideal for experienced electricians and technicians who are working at a higher technical level – for example, those involved in design, coordination, inspection & testing, commissioning, complex fault-finding, or acting as a lead technician on site. If you are moving beyond basic installation work, the Technician Gold Card helps you show that progression to employers and clients.

Application Process with Apply ECS Card

  1. Check that you meet the Technician-level qualification and experience criteria.
  2. Book and pass the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment if your current pass has expired.
  3. Use our online form to provide your personal details, qualifications and work history.
  4. Upload your certificates, ECS HSE pass, photo ID and passport-style photograph.
  5. We review your information and submit a complete application to ECS on your behalf.
  6. ECS process, approve and issue your ECS Technician Gold Card, which is then posted to your UK address.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the ECS Technician Gold Card valid for?

The ECS Technician Gold Card is normally valid for 3 years. After this, you will need to renew the card, including making sure your ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment is still in date.

How much does it cost through Apply ECS Card?

Our service fee is £79 for handling your ECS Technician Gold Card application. Please note that ECS may charge additional fees for the card itself and for the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental test.

Do I need NVQ/SVQ Level 3 to get the Technician Gold Card?

Yes, in most cases you will need at least an NVQ/SVQ Level 3 or an equivalent recognised Technician-level qualification in an electrotechnical discipline, plus relevant experience.

Can I upgrade from another ECS card to the Technician Gold Card?

If you currently hold another ECS card and have since gained higher qualifications and experience, you may be able to upgrade to the Technician Gold Card. Apply ECS Card can review your situation and help you choose the correct application route.
