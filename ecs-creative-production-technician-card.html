Eligibility Requirements

  • Experience in creative production, technical production, staging or AV roles.
  • Good understanding of production health, safety and site rules.
  • Currently working in, or seeking work within, UK creative production environments.
  • Successful completion of the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment.
  • Ability to work safely on construction, commercial or event-based production sites.

Required Documentation

  • Proof of identity (Passport or UK Driving Licence).
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement dated within the last 3 months).
  • Evidence of creative production or technical production experience (CV, contracts, references, etc.).
  • Recent passport-style photograph.
  • ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment pass certificate (if available).

Application Process

  1. Apply through this website – we guide you and provide a free mock test to help you prepare for the ECS assessment.
  2. Provide your personal details and information about your production or technical experience.
  3. Pass the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment at an approved test centre.
  4. Submit evidence of creative production or technical work (experience or training).
  5. We review your documents and submit your ECS Creative Production Technician application for verification.
  6. Your ECS card is produced and dispatched – usually within 15–20 working days after approval.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the ECS Creative Production Technician Card application take?

Once we receive your completed application and all supporting documents, it typically takes 15–20 working days for your ECS Creative Production Technician Card to be processed and dispatched. Incomplete applications can take longer to approve.

What is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment?

This is a mandatory safety test covering key health, safety and environmental topics for UK sites. You must pass this assessment before your ECS card can be issued, and it is usually valid for the life of the card.

What technical skills does this card validate?

The ECS Creative Production Technician Card validates skills such as equipment setup and operation, basic electrical and AV handling (where applicable), stage or production rigging support, troubleshooting technical issues and maintaining safe working practices in production environments.

How do I renew my Creative Production Technician card?

You can usually renew your card up to 6 months before its expiry date. The process is similar to your first application and may require updating your details and, in some cases, retaking the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment.
