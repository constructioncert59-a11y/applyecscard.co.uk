Professional Winding Certification

The ECS Electrical Winder Card is for qualified electrical winders who work on the winding, repair and servicing of electrical machinery such as motors, generators and transformers. This card demonstrates competency in winding techniques, fault finding and safe working practices in line with UK health & safety standards.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Relevant experience in electrical winding, rewinding or servicing of electrical machinery
  • Completed and passed the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment
  • Currently working in the UK electrical or engineering industry
  • Typically at least 3 years’ experience in electrical winding roles
  • Valid proof of identity (Passport, Driving Licence or Government-issued ID)

Required Documentation

  • Completed ECS application form
  • Proof of a valid ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment
  • Evidence of electrical winding experience (employment reference, job role details, etc.)
  • One recent passport-size photograph
  • Proof of ID (Passport or Driving Licence)

Training & Courses

Approved UK training providers offer electrical winding and machinery repair courses, along with the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment. These can be completed through registered training centres before applying for your ECS Electrical Winder Card.

Application Process

  1. Confirm you have suitable experience in electrical winding and machinery servicing.
  2. Pass the required ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment.
  3. Gather all required documentation (ID, photo, proof of assessment, experience evidence).
  4. Submit your application online through ApplyECSCard or via your employer/training provider.
  5. We review your details and submit your ECS application for processing.
  6. Receive your ECS Electrical Winder Card – usually within 10–15 working days once approved.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Electrical Winder Card application take?

Once we receive your completed application and all supporting documents, it typically takes around 10–15 working days for your ECS Electrical Winder Card to be processed and dispatched. Delays can occur if information is missing or incorrect.

What is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment?

This is a mandatory test covering key health, safety and environmental topics for UK sites. You must pass this assessment before your ECS Electrical Winder Card can be issued, and it normally remains valid for the life of the card.

How long is the Electrical Winder Card valid for?

The ECS Electrical Winder Card is typically valid for 3 years. After this, you will need to renew or extend your card and may need to retake the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment.

What machinery does this card cover?

This card is suitable for winders working on a wide range of electrical equipment, including motors, generators, transformers and other rotating electrical machinery that requires winding, rewinding or specialised servicing.
