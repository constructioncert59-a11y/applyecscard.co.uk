Professional Certification

The ECS Creative Production Operative Card provides formal recognition of your skills in creative, media and production work carried out on UK construction, commercial and event sites. It confirms that you understand safe working practices, site rules and health & safety expectations in creative production environments.

Holding this ECS card helps you demonstrate competence to employers, main contractors, production companies and site managers, supporting access to more opportunities in creative and event-based roles.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Experience in creative production, media or related roles (events, exhibitions, stage, AV, film or TV support).
  • Good understanding of production safety principles and safe working practices.
  • Currently working in, or actively seeking work in, creative production environments.
  • Successful completion of the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment (where required).
  • Ability to follow site rules, risk assessments and safety briefings on UK sites.

Required Documentation

  • Proof of identity (valid passport or photocard driving licence).
  • Proof of address (dated within the last 3 months, such as a utility bill or bank statement).
  • Evidence of creative production or media experience (CV, references or role descriptions).
  • Recent passport-style photograph that meets ID requirements.
  • ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment certificate (if applicable).

Application Process

  1. Apply through this website and get a free mock test to help you prepare and pass your ECS assessment with confidence.
  2. Provide your personal details and information about your creative production experience.
  3. Book and pass the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment (if not already completed).
  4. Upload your supporting documents, including proof of ID, address, experience and photograph.
  5. Your details and documentation are verified and your application is submitted for ECS approval.
  6. Once approved, your ECS Creative Production Operative Card is produced and dispatched within 15–20 working days.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

Once we receive your completed application and all required documents, it typically takes 15–20 working days to process and dispatch your card. If information is missing or unclear, this may delay the process.

What is the ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment?

The ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment checks your understanding of site safety, environmental responsibilities and working rules on UK construction, commercial and event sites. It is normally required before an ECS Creative Production Operative Card can be issued.

What types of creative production work does this card cover?

This card can be used for a wide range of creative roles including events production, exhibition and stage setup, AV and media operations, film and television support, live performance support and other on-site creative production activities.

How do I renew my card before expiry?

You can normally renew your ECS Creative Production Operative Card up to six months before it expires. The renewal process is similar to a new application and may require updated evidence of experience and a valid ECS Health, Safety & Environmental Assessment, depending on current ECS rules at the time of renewal.
