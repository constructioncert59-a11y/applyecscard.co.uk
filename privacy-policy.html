Apply ECS Card – Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Apply ECS Card is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. This policy explains how we collect, process, store, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

What Information We Collect

When you apply for or book an ECS Test or ECS Card through our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • UK address and postcode
  • Telephone number
  • Email address
  • National Insurance number
  • IP address
  • ECS-related identifiers for test or card processing

How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Processing ECS test bookings
  • Processing ECS card applications
  • Providing customer support and communication regarding your booking
  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

We do not use your information for marketing unless you provide explicit consent.

Lawful Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following lawful bases under UK GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract (to process your ECS test or ECS card booking)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate interests (to operate and improve our services)

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections (rectification) of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Receive a copy of your information in electronic format (data portability)

To exercise these rights, please email us at booking@applyecscard.co.uk.

Payments

All payments are securely processed through accredited third-party payment gateways. Apply ECS Card does not store and cannot access your full card details at any time.

Data Sharing

We may share relevant information only with authorised ECS-related service providers for the purpose of processing your booking, such as:

  • Pearson VUE – official ECS test provider
  • HSS Training – approved test & training partner

We never sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete your ECS test or ECS card booking
  • Provide after-sales support
  • Meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements

In general, this is up to 6 months after service completion, unless a longer retention period is required by law. After this period, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

Data Security

Your information is stored on secure, encrypted servers and is accessible only to authorised staff members who need it to perform their duties. We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, or alteration.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve functionality, security, and user experience, and to understand how our website is used. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not function properly.

Third-Party Websites

Our site may include links to third-party websites. Apply ECS Card is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of those external sites. We recommend that you review their privacy policies before submitting any personal data.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can remove the information.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that may affect your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as soon as reasonably possible and, where required, report the incident to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) in the UK.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, regulation, or our services. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date.
