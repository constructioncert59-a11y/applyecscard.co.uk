Before you book your CITB test

This form is for applicants who need a CITB Health, Safety & Environment (HS&E) test as part of an ECS card application, renewal or upgrade.

Across the UK there are 50+ official CITB HS&E test centre areas where you can sit a test that is accepted for ECS card routes.

  • Check which ECS card and level you are working towards.
  • Make sure you know which CITB HS&E test is required.
  • Have your National Insurance number and ID details ready.
  • Choose a UK test centre that is easy for you to reach.

On the day of your test

CITB HS&E tests follow strict rules so that your result can be accepted for ECS card applications.

  • Bring valid, original photo ID (for example a passport or UK driving licence).
  • Arrive at least 15 minutes before your scheduled start time.
  • Electronic devices must be switched off and stored as instructed.
  • Follow the instructions given by test centre staff at all times.
